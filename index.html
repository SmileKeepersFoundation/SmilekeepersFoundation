About Us

“We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give”

An NGO which is the embodiment of the above is the Smile Keepers Foundation in Goa . It is a youth centric NGO committed to bring about a positive change in the society, uniting everyone towards a common cause, that is humanity.
Smilekeepers Foundation is backed by a team of dedicated youths, who work with grassroot initiatives for effecting positive changes in the lives of underprivileged children, their families and communities.
Our NGO indulges in activities that include distribution of food and clothes, we help in creating a better learning environment for the children in slums. We are also involved with the development of a school in Gadag, Karnataka. Mission Apne and Mission Sath is our foundation's crusade to provide food and basic amenities to the people in need. We have helped over 5,000 people in the last 3 years as part of our mission.

We are determined to help as many people as possible keeping in mind that every act of kindness is charity. Our ultimate goal is to spread love and smiles across Goa. All we wish is to make this world a happy place. In the coming years we wish to grow this organization by letting people know about our vision and motive.
Mother Teresa once said, “Not all of us can do great things, but we can do small things with great love”. We often think that we are too small to make a change but encouraging an act of kindness towards underprivileged is also a great act of love. Smile Keepers is working day and night to help people have a healthy life with regular meals a day and shelter over their head with a peaceful sleep at night.
Join us in our path of spreading smiles. Give us your best wishes to achieve success in our duty.
